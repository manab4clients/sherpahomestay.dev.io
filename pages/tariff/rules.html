Rules for a Comfortable Stay

To ensure a pleasant and hassle-free experience at Jhandi Sherpa Home Stay, we request all guests to kindly follow the guidelines below:

  • Check-in time is 12:00 PM, and check-out time is 10:00 AM.
  • Each room accommodates up to four guests. Additional guests are not permitted without prior approval.
  • Meals are served as per the schedule. Please follow the timings for a smooth dining experience.
  • Room heaters are available for an extra charge of ₹700/- and can be used from 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM.
  • Hot water is available through geysers between 7:30–9:00 AM and 8:00–9:00 PM.
  • Payments can be made via CASH OR ONLINE. Full payment is required during check-in.
  • Please maintain cleanliness and handle all property items with care; damages will incur charges.

Please take a moment to read through our detailed Terms & Conditions to ensure a smooth and enjoyable stay.
